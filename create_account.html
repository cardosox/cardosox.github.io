How to Delete Your Account in Be The Manager

Welcome to the account deletion page for Be The Manager, developed by Mobisoca.

You can delete your account directly from within the app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Be The Manager app on your device.
  2. Select the Online mode when prompted.
  3. Choose Setup Match.
  4. Complete the login process if prompted, or continue with silent sign-in.
  5. Tap the floating action button labeled Actions at the bottom of the screen.
  6. Select Delete Account from the menu options.
