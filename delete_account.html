How to Delete Your Account in Be the Manager

You can delete your account directly from within the Be the Manager. Once you enter the app and select the Online mode, you'll be prompted to choose between Match Setup or My Team Builder.

After choosing Match Setup and completing the login (or silent sign-in), you will see a white floating action button at the bottom right of the screen. Tap this button to expand the menu, and among the options, you'll find Delete Account.

Data Deletion

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (including email address and user ID)
  • Your game progress, including custom teams, match history, and other related data
  • Any other personal data stored in the app

Some data, such as app usage logs or anonymous performance statistics, may be retained for compliance or analytical purposes, but this data is not tied to your personal information.
